## Review your performance and your team's performance

### Your performance

This section offers an overview of your performance results and efforts. Here you can:

-   Review your recent feedback. It can help you prepare a self review or a promotion case. The performance feed lists all your reviews and feedback in chronological order.
-   Find specific feedback to identify areas of improvement or keep track of your best efforts. Use the filters at the top of the page.
-   Know at a glance what requires your attention: unfinished feedback drafts, and feedback requests.

### Team performance

If you are a supervisor, you can access an overview of your team (and your entire reporting line) under Team performance. Here, you can:

-   Identify opportunities for coaching and development. Check each team member's latest  [performance and potential ratings](https://support.personio.de/hc/en-us/articles/26260833891101).
-   Prepare before performance meetings or calibration discussions. If you click a team member, you see an overview of their performance: their latest cycle reviews, feedback, and  [performance notes](https://support.personio.de/hc/en-us/articles/209361385). If you activate it, the  [AI functionality](https://support.personio.de/hc/en-us/articles/30612613685789)  provides a summary.
