# Product update note

## Context
A short snippet to introduce a product improvement. 

## Screenshot
![Overview article](/assets/images/product-update-note.png)

## Excerpt

Performance

*   We introduced new widgets for easier access to cycle and performance data.
    
*   Under [Cycle insights](https://web.archive.org/web/20260308130635/https://support.personio.de/hc/en-us/articles/27918461836317), the Question Comparison graph now displays all question types, for a complete analysis of cycle results.
    
*   Admins can extend [activity due dates](https://web.archive.org/web/20260308130635/https://support.personio.de/hc/en-us/articles/4415703772561) even after the activity has ended, for greater flexibility.
    
*   New icons provide a quick visual indicator when reviews are submitted or shared.
    
*   You can apply color-coding to [performance ratings](https://web.archive.org/web/20260308130635/https://support.personio.de/hc/en-us/articles/26260833891101), making them easier to read and interpret.
